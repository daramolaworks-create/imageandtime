The Problem

Water had no personality

Every brand said the same thing: pure, clean, healthy. Nestle Pure Life was being outspent by competitors saying identical things louder. The brand had trust but no distinctiveness.

The Insight

People do not buy water — they buy the moment

Nobody thinks about water itself. They think about the feeling after a run, the relief in Lagos heat, the quiet pause in a busy day. Water is the backdrop to real life moments.

The Idea

Make everyday moments feel refreshed

We repositioned Nestle Pure Life as the brand of quiet confidence — the one that shows up in the moments that matter without shouting about it.
