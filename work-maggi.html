Work / Food & Activation

You have got the recipe.

A belief-led campaign connecting individuality with the everyday magic of making good food.

The brief

Start a project

Maggi needed a campaign platform that went beyond seasoning and spoke to something bigger: the talent and confidence people bring to the kitchen every day.

"You have got the recipe" became the rallying cry — a celebration of personal expression through food.

01

Campaign platform

A single creative idea flexible enough to work across TV, digital, retail and social.

02

Food storytelling

Visual and verbal language that made everyday cooking feel aspirational.

03

Activation design

On-ground experiences and retail touchpoints that brought the platform to life.

04

Social content

A content system designed for participation and community-driven recipe culture.

The Problem

Seasoning had become invisible

Maggi was everywhere but ubiquity had made it wallpaper. People used it without thinking. The brand had presence but no meaning, no emotional pull, no reason to be chosen with pride.

The Insight

Cooking is an act of self-expression

For Nigerian cooks, a meal is never just food. It is a signature, a reputation, a statement. That personal pride was the emotional territory Maggi had never claimed.

The Idea

You already have what it takes

Maggi became the enabler of talent that was already there — not the hero of the dish, but the partner of the cook who already had the recipe inside them.
