La Casera

Bold refreshment cues built for visibility, appetite and cultural recall.

The brief

Start a project

La Casera needed campaign work that matched the boldness of its brand personality. The brief was to create refreshment cues strong enough to cut through in a competitive beverage market.

01

Campaign design

A visual system built for instant recognition across outdoor, retail and digital.

02

Colour and composition

Bold palette and layout rules that made the brand impossible to miss.

03

Cultural recall

Messaging rooted in celebration and the social moments people associate with refreshment.

04

Activation assets

On-ground materials, event branding and sampling experiences that drove trial.

The Problem

Refreshment had become a visual commodity

Every soft drink brand used the same playbook: condensation on a bottle, pouring shots, smiling faces. La Casera looked like everything else on the shelf and outdoors.

The Insight

Refreshment is social, not solitary

Nigerians do not drink alone. La Casera shows up at parties, family gatherings, celebrations. The real territory was the energy of the occasion it belonged to.

The Idea

Own the moment, not the category

We built a campaign system around the energy of Nigerian social life — colour, composition and messaging designed to make La Casera feel like part of every gathering worth attending.
